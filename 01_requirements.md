Project scope
Domain: Simple e‑commerce customer profitability analysis.​

Entities: Customer, Product, Date, Sales/Transactions, Profitability metrics.​

Main metrics: Revenue, cost, profit, profit margin (profit ÷ revenue).​

Main outputs:

Profit per customer over a date range.​

Top N most profitable customers.​

Profit by customer segment (e.g., Regular, Premium, SME).
